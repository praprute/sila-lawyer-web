About Us

Ex injuria jus non oritur.

Law does not arise from injustice

Siam International Legal Advisors (SILA) is a full-service law firm based in Bangkok, Thailand. The firm also serves clients in other business areas such as Phuket, Chaingmai and Samui. SILA was established in 2010, bringing together a team of premier attorneys with decades of experience. SILA is structured to provide sophisticated legal representation across a range of practice areas ranging from corporate law, intellectual property, employment and estate planning to all types of family law, civil and criminal litigation. Individuals and businesses can consult us whether they are filing bankruptcy, need assistance with business formation, or have other legal concerns.
Each attorney at SILA recognizes that every client has distinctive professional and personal legal needs that change from time to time, and require the committed personal attention of experienced attorneys. As a full-service law firm, SILA is committed to results, featuring client-centered practice areas specifically designed to address diverse and overlapping legal issues.

Practice Areas

At vero eos et accusamus et iusto odio dignissimos ducimus qui blanditiis praesentium

Litigation

      On general commercial litigation matters, SILA has significant clients in a variety of industries including construction, jewelry, recruitment, shipping, aviation, exercise equipment, computer, consulting, design and marketing companies, advertisement agencies, web based business, pharmaceutical, banking, digital marketing, publishing, real estate and textile industries. SILA also represents real estate owners, brokers, developers and executors and administrators of estates.


Corporate

       SILA knows that executives need outside counsel with the ability to help anticipate and address legal issues on every level, from day-to-day corporate counseling and securities law compliance, to intellectual property, tax, and employment law and benefits matters to complex boardroom situations, mergers and acquisitions or sophisticated cross-border undertakings. Therefore, our lawyers have been trained to provide efficient and accessible advice spanning the full suite of practice areas encountered by public and private company executives. Our deep experience representing clients across a diverse range of industries permits us to offer practical counseling with a keen focus on the business’ bottom line.


Criminal

       SILA has broad experience in the field of criminal litigation. Members of our criminal practice group have brought their own unique expertise along with a significant amount of trial experience.
As each member contributes his or her own unique skills to the practice group, our success has grown significantly through the free exchange of ideas and experiences. SILA has enjoyed success at many levels of the court system including the Court of Appeal and the Supreme Court. With criminal litigation growing more and more complex, you can count on our team to be up to date and ready for your case.


Real Estate

       SILA has experience in virtually all real estate and development matters. We represent clients in a broad range of sophisticated and complex real estate transactions and offer a full range of services related to the purchase, sale, leasing, development, financing, joint venture, syndication, securitization, use and management of real property and real estate portfolios. Our representations include work relating to lease agreements, financing and loan documents, restructurings and workouts, condemnation, eminent domain, valuation and tax issues, purchase and sale agreement negotiations, real estate development and real estate portfolio management. With experience in many real estate market cycles, we understand the challenges in both the up-cycles and downturns and have the depth and experience necessary to advise clients on a broad range of sophisticated issues. Our experience includes handling the routine issues involved in real estate matters, as well as determining solutions to complex and seemingly unsolvable dilemmas that require solid working relationships within the industry and a high level of understanding of the nuances of deals, regulations, profitability, leverage and liability protection. We place primary emphasis on finding practical and creative solutions to facilitate our clients' business objectives and closing transactions both advantageously and efficiently.


Intellectual Property

       In today’s increasingly competitive global marketplace, where ever-changing technologies and accelerated product development are the norm, growing and maintaining your intellectual property (IP) portfolio and protecting your company’s investments and innovations are critical. Whether you are a global business operating around the world or an individual entrepreneur with an innovative idea, developing, managing and protecting your intellectual property portfolio is essential to assure your long-term success.
SILA’s intellectual property team provides a complete range of legal services designed to help you protect your innovations and succeed in a truly competitive market. With a full understanding of the issues and challenges our clients face and extensive experience addressing all forms of intellectual property law, we are well-positioned to effectively and cost-efficiently protect your interests.


Immigration

       The attorneys of SILA’s immigration practice group limit their practices exclusively to immigration law, a focus essential given the complexity and changeability of Thailand immigration laws and regulations. Our attorneys advise clients internationally on the widest possible range of immigration issues, including bringing foreign workers into Thailand, protecting the legal status of foreign workers already inside Thailand, and sending domestic workers abroad to staff foreign offices.
In an age of increased immigration enforcement, our attorneys work with clients to ensure comprehensive compliance with Thai immigration laws. This includes, but is by no means limited to, conducting precautionary audits of immigration documentation (such as Tor. Mor. 7, 8 and 47) and devising company-wide policies to ensure legal work authorization of workers.
